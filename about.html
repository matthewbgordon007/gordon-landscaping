About

Gordon Landscaping Company is your one-stop shop for exterior work across Ottawa and the surrounding area — sod, interlock, turf, decks, fencing, and custom outdoor structures, all handled by one team.

Meet Matthew

Owner, Gordon Landscaping Company

I built this company to be the one call for everything outside your home. Whether it's a new fence, a deck, interlock, sod, or a custom carport — one crew handles it all so your yard comes together as a complete transformation, not a patchwork of contractors.

What you can expect

You’re hiring us for a result that looks sharp now and still looks sharp later. That means doing the not-so-fun stuff properly: base work, grading, and the finishing details.

  • • Clear scope and timelines
  • • Proper base work and grading
  • • Jobsite kept clean and organized
  • • Crisp edges, level lines, tight seams
  • • Straight answers and pro communication
