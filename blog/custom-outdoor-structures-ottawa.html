Custom Outdoor Structures in Ottawa: Carports, Pergolas & More

Custom outdoor structures turn a yard into a true extension of your home. From carports to pergolas, here's a look at the options, what affects cost, and how a custom build comes together in the Ottawa area.

Popular outdoor structures

  • Carports — protect vehicles from sun, snow, and ice with a custom timber-frame and metal-roof build.
  • Pergolas — define a patio or seating area and add shade and architectural interest.
  • Gazebos & pavilions — a covered destination in the yard for dining and entertaining.
  • Privacy screens & feature walls — block sightlines and add structure to an outdoor space.

What affects the cost

  • Size and span — larger structures need more material and heavier framing.
  • Materials and finish — timber species, roofing, stain, and hardware all factor in.
  • Foundations — frost-depth footings keep the structure stable year-round.
  • Design complexity — exposed trusses, custom rooflines, and detailing add craftsmanship and time.

Built to integrate with your property

The best custom builds look like they belong. We design structures that match your home and landscape — proportions, materials, and finishes that fit the setting rather than stand out awkwardly.

Permits and code

Many outdoor structures require a permit in Ottawa depending on size and use. We build to code with proper footings and framing so your structure is safe and lasts.
