Sod Installation in Ottawa: What to Expect

If you want a lawn that looks clean right away and stays healthy, the biggest difference is prep. Here’s what actually matters on a sod job in Ottawa.

1) Prep and grading matter more than the sod

The nicest sod in the world won’t fix a bumpy grade or poor drainage. Good installs start with a smooth grade and the right soil base so water moves properly and the lawn roots quickly.

2) Topsoil and soil quality

In many Ottawa yards, soil can be compacted or thin. A fresh topsoil layer (as needed) helps the sod establish and keeps it looking even across the whole area.

3) Timing in Ottawa

Spring through fall is common for sod installation. The key isn’t the month, it’s committing to watering right after install — especially in hot weeks.

4) Watering schedule (simple version)

  • • First 7–10 days: keep it consistently moist (don’t let it dry out)
  • • Weeks 2–3: water deeper, less often to encourage rooting
  • • Avoid heavy traffic for the first 2–3 weeks
