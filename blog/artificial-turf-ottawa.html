Artificial Turf in Ottawa: Pros, Cons & Drainage

Turf can be a great fit in Ottawa — but only if the base and drainage are done right. Here’s the simple breakdown.

When turf makes sense

  • • Shady yards where grass struggles
  • • High-traffic areas that turn to mud
  • • Low-maintenance backyards
  • • Clean transitions with interlock

What to avoid

The biggest mistake is skipping prep. Turf needs a stable, compacted base and a plan for drainage so water doesn’t pool.

Why drainage is everything

Ottawa weather means freeze/thaw, heavy rain, and melt cycles. A proper base helps turf stay flat, drain properly, and look clean long-term.
