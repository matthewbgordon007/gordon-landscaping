Interlock • Ottawa

Interlock Patios & Walkways in Ottawa

Interlock is only as good as the base. We focus on proper excavation, compaction, and clean cuts for a premium finish that lasts.

What we build

  • • Interlock patios (backyards, seating areas)
  • • Walkways and front entrances
  • • Pool surrounds (clean cuts + drainage considerations)
  • • Borders and clean transitions into sod/turf

Our process

  1. 1) Layout: confirm shape, elevations, transitions, and drainage.
  2. 2) Excavation: dig to spec based on use and conditions.
  3. 3) Base build: granular base + compaction in lifts.
  4. 4) Install: pattern alignment, cuts, and leveling.
  5. 5) Finish: joint sand (polymeric optional), final clean-up.

What affects price

  • • Square footage
  • • Stone choice + pattern
  • • Excavation + disposal
  • • Access
  • • Edge details + steps

Interlock FAQ

How much does it cost?

Depends on size, materials, base build, access, and design details. We quote accurately after seeing the site/photos.

Why is base work so important?

A proper base reduces settling, shifting, and freeze/thaw issues over time.

Can you do interlock around a pool?

Yes. We plan cuts, edges, and drainage so it looks clean and performs safely.

Do you use polymeric sand?

Optional depending on the project. We’ll recommend the best finish for your space.
