Interlock Patio

Ottawa, ON

The Project

A residential interlock patio installation focused on precision and longevity. The homeowner wanted a surface that looked professional and stayed level through freeze-thaw cycles.

What We Did

  • Level lines: Careful layout and grading for a flat, even surface.
  • Tight joints: Consistent joint spacing and proper polymeric sand for stability.
  • Pattern selection: Chosen paver layout to suit the space and avoid awkward cuts.
