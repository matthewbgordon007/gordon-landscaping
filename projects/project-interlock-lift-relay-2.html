Interlock Lift & Relay

Ottawa, ON

The Project

A settled interlock driveway and walkway needed repair. The pavers had shifted over time due to improper base compaction and poor drainage. We performed a full lift and relay to restore the surface without replacing the materials.

What We Did

  • Assessment: Identified soft spots, drainage issues, and areas of settlement.
  • Base correction: Improved grading, added base material where needed, compacted properly.
  • Relay & finish: Reinstalled pavers level, re-sanded joints, and ensured proper slope for drainage.
