Rustic Wooden Carport

Ottawa area

The Project

A fully custom timber-frame carport built to shelter vehicles while harmonizing with a wooded Ottawa property. The homeowner wanted a structure that felt intentional—not an afterthought—so we designed around existing trees and sight lines.

What We Built

  • Timber frame: Exposed trusses and beams for a rustic, architectural look that ages well in the elements.
  • Metal roof: Durable, low-maintenance metal roof with proper slope for snow shedding and drainage.
  • Foundation & posts: Proper footings and support to handle Ottawa winters and long-term stability.
