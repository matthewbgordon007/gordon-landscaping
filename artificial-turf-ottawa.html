Our process

  1. 1) Excavation: remove material and prep for a stable base.
  2. 2) Base build: compacted aggregate for stability and drainage.
  3. 3) Turf install: seams aligned, secured, and trimmed clean.
  4. 4) Edge details: crisp transitions into interlock, gardens, or borders.
  5. 5) Finish: infill + grooming for a natural look and performance.

Artificial turf FAQ

How much does turf cost?

Depends on prep, base, access, drainage, and edging details. We quote accurately with photos and rough measurements.

Does it need drainage?

Yes — base prep and drainage planning are key for Ottawa weather.

Is it good for dogs?

It can be, depending on your setup. We can recommend options based on your yard and use.

How long does turf last?

Depends on product quality and use. Proper install and base prep matter most.
