Sod Installation in Ottawa

A great lawn starts with prep. We focus on grading, topsoil, and tight seams so your sod establishes fast and looks clean.

Our process

  1. 1) Site prep: remove/level as needed and set the area up for a clean finish.
  2. 2) Grading: correct slope so water moves away and the lawn stays smooth.
  3. 3) Topsoil: install/condition soil for healthy rooting and even growth.
  4. 4) Install: tight seams, clean edges, and rolling for a smooth surface.
  5. 5) Watering plan: simple instructions so it establishes quickly.

What affects price

  • • Topsoil depth/quality

Sod installation FAQ

How much does it cost?

It depends on prep, access, grading, topsoil, and total area. We quote accurately once we see photos/dimensions.

Best time of year?

Spring through fall. Watering is the key to quick rooting, especially during hot weeks.

How long to establish?

Usually 2–3 weeks for initial rooting. Avoid heavy use during that time.

Do you handle grading/topsoil?

Yes — proper prep is a big part of how we get a clean, long-lasting lawn.
